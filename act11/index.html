Bachelor In Applied Computer Technology

The Bact is four year degree program that focuses on the practical aspect of the science of computing

The program develops core skill in multimedia technology,web moblie and netwerk systems administration.

Futhermore it prepares learners with supervisor and manangement skills.The currculum is derrived form the

BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Tech with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) no.31 serias of 2021

Program Objectives:

The program prepare learners with the practical skill in multimedia technology,
web mobile development and network system administration and supervisional skill
to manage an organization in this areas of competrencies,
engage in a startup business or pursue a teachin career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able :
1.Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being a ethical computing professional when making work-related decision.
2.Develops product or processes br applying thehir knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
3.Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes of products.
4.Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
5.Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunties.
